Women music educators and Australian honours

This list of Australian women music educators who have received honours for service to music education was extracted from the Australian Honours database in October 2019, and is up-to-date at that time. We searched on the word 'music' in the citation field, and noted women cited for services to music education or music teaching. Imperial honours citations sometimes did not state the reason for the citation, so this search may not have picked up all Australian women music educators who have received honours under the Imperial honours system. Please let us know of omissions.

We believe recognising the valuable contributions of women to Australia is important, and encourage you to #NominateAWoman - visit the Australian Government It's an Honour website and Honour a Woman for more information.
